What You Can Do on AIRnote

Our Vision

We believe people trust real people more than advertisements. AIRnote is built to encourage honest sharing and meaningful guidance between users.

Community & Safety

AIRnote has strict community rules. Content is not allowed if it includes:

  • Sexual or adult material
  • Hate speech, violence, or harassment
  • Illegal activities
  • Spam, scams, or misleading information

Users can report content and accounts. Our team will review reports and take appropriate action when necessary.

Free & Ad-Free

AIRnote is currently completely free to use. There are no advertisements and no paid features at this time.
