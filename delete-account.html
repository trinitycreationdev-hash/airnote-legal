How to Delete Your AIRnote Account

AIRnote allows all users to permanently delete their account and associated data directly from within the app. No external form or email request is required.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the AIRnote app
  2. Go to your Profile screen
  3. Open Settings
  4. Tap on Delete Account
  5. Confirm your choice

Once confirmed, your account will be scheduled for deletion.

What Data Is Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data will be removed:

  • Your profile information (username, bio, photos)
  • Your notes and uploaded images
  • Your likes and interactions
  • Your authentication account

Data Retention Period

All personal data will be permanently deleted from our servers within 7 days after account deletion, unless longer retention is required by law.
