Account Management

Delete Your Account

You can permanently delete your AIRnote account and all associated data at any time. This page explains how to do it and what happens to your data.

Operated By Trinity Creation
Data Deleted Within 30 days of request
Contact trinitycreation.dev@gmail.com

Before You Delete

Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.

Once you confirm deletion, your account, posts, and all associated data will be permanently removed. There is no way to recover your account or content after this action is completed.

If you are experiencing an issue with AIRnote, consider contacting us first at trinitycreation.dev@gmail.com before deleting your account. We may be able to help.

How to Delete Your Account

Account deletion is done entirely within the AIRnote app. No email request or external form is required.

1
Open the AIRnote app

Make sure you are signed in to the account you want to delete.

2
Go to your Profile

Tap the Profile icon in the bottom navigation bar.

3
Open Settings

Tap the Settings icon in the top right corner of your profile.

4
Tap "Delete Account"

Scroll to the bottom of Settings and tap Delete Account.

5
Confirm your decision

Read the confirmation message and confirm. Your account will be scheduled for permanent deletion immediately.

What Happens to Your Data

Here is exactly what is deleted and what may be temporarily retained after you delete your account.

Data Type What Happens
Profile (username, bio, photo) Permanently deleted within 30 days
Posts and text content Removed from public view immediately. Permanently deleted within 30 days
Uploaded images Permanently deleted within 30 days
Comments and replies Permanently deleted within 30 days
Likes and interactions Permanently deleted within 30 days
Following and followers list Permanently deleted within 30 days
Authentication account Permanently deleted within 30 days
Backup and system logs May be retained temporarily in encrypted backups. Purged within 60 days.
Legal or safety records Retained only where required by Indian law or for ongoing legal/safety investigations.

Your posts are removed from public view immediately upon account deletion. Other users will no longer be able to see your content from the moment you confirm deletion. Full data removal from all systems is completed within 30 days.

Can't Access Your Account?

If you are unable to log in to your account and want it deleted, contact us directly and we will handle the deletion manually.
