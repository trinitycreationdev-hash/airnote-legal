AIRnote Child Safety Standards

Effective Date: 13 May 2026

AIRnote is committed to creating a safe, respectful, and responsible platform for all users.


We have zero tolerance for any form of child sexual abuse, exploitation, grooming, or harmful behavior involving minors.

1. Minimum Age Requirement

AIRnote is intended only for users aged 13 years and older.


Accounts found to belong to users under 13 may be restricted, suspended, or permanently removed.

2. Zero Tolerance Policy

The following content and behavior are strictly prohibited:


  • Sexual content involving minors
  • Child sexual abuse material (CSAM)
  • Grooming or exploitation of children
  • Requests for explicit images involving minors
  • Harassment or predatory behavior toward minors
  • Sharing or promoting harmful child exploitation content
  • Attempts to contact minors for exploitative purposes

3. Reporting & Safety Tools

AIRnote provides reporting systems that allow users to report:


  • Notes
  • Comments and replies
  • User profiles
  • Inappropriate or harmful behavior

Reported content may be reviewed by moderation systems and appropriate actions may be taken.

4. Moderation & Enforcement

AIRnote may take actions including:


  • Removing violating content
  • Restricting accounts
  • Permanent bans
  • Reporting illegal activity to relevant authorities when required

We reserve the right to act immediately on content or accounts that may threaten child safety.

5. Community Safety

AIRnote encourages respectful and safe interactions between users.


Harassment, abusive behavior, exploitation, or inappropriate conduct involving minors is strictly prohibited.

6. Cooperation With Authorities

AIRnote may cooperate with law enforcement and child protection organizations where legally required.


Illegal child exploitation content may be reported to appropriate authorities.
