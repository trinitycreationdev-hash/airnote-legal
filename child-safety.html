AIRnote Child Safety Standards

Effective Date: 28/01/2026

AIRnote is committed to maintaining a safe and respectful community. We have zero tolerance for any form of child sexual abuse, exploitation, or harmful behaviour involving minors.

1. Age Requirement

AIRnote is intended for users aged 13 and above. Accounts found to belong to users under 13 may be removed.

2. Prohibited Content

The following content is strictly prohibited:

  • Sexual content involving minors
  • Grooming or exploitation of children
  • Requests for sexual images or personal contact with minors
  • Any illegal or abusive content involving children

3. Reporting System

Users can report notes that violate community and child safety guidelines directly within the app. Reported notes are reviewed and may be removed if they violate safety rules.

4. Moderation and Enforcement

AIRnote reviews reported content and may remove notes or restrict accounts that violate safety rules. We cooperate with legal authorities when required by law.
