01   Our Commitment

AIRnote is unconditionally committed to child safety. The protection of children is not a policy preference — it is a non-negotiable principle that governs every decision we make as a platform.

AIRnote is a real-time experience sharing platform operated by Trinity Creation. We understand that real-time, location-based social platforms carry serious responsibility. We take that responsibility seriously.

We are committed to maintaining a platform that is safe, respectful, and completely free from any content or behaviour that could harm, exploit, or endanger children in any way.

02   Minimum Age Requirement

AIRnote is intended exclusively for users who are 13 years of age or older.

By registering an account, users confirm that they meet this minimum age requirement. Users between the ages of 13 and 17 are considered minors and are subject to additional protections under this policy.

If we discover or are informed that an account belongs to a user under the age of 13, we will:

  • Immediately suspend the account
  • Remove any content associated with the account
  • Permanently delete the account and associated data

Parents or guardians who believe their child under 13 has created an AIRnote account should contact us immediately at trinitycreation.dev@gmail.com . We will act promptly.

03   Zero Tolerance Policy

AIRnote has absolute zero tolerance for child sexual abuse material (CSAM), child exploitation, grooming, or any form of content or conduct that endangers minors.

Violations will result in immediate permanent account termination and will be reported to law enforcement authorities and relevant child protection organisations without exception.

This policy applies to all users regardless of age, location, account history, or any other factor. There are no exceptions, warnings, or second chances for violations of this nature.

04   Prohibited Content & Conduct

The following content and behaviour are strictly and absolutely prohibited on AIRnote:

Sexual Content Involving Minors

  • Child sexual abuse material (CSAM) of any kind
  • Sexually explicit content involving anyone under the age of 18
  • Content that sexualises, objectifies, or fetishises minors in any way
  • Drawn, animated, illustrated, or AI-generated sexual content involving minors
  • Sharing, distributing, or linking to any of the above

Grooming & Exploitation

  • Any attempt to establish inappropriate relationships with minors
  • Grooming behaviour including building excessive trust with a minor for exploitative purposes
  • Requesting, soliciting, or encouraging minors to share personal information, images, or meet in person
  • Using AIRnote's location features to identify, target, or track minors
  • Any form of coercion, manipulation, or pressure directed at a minor

Harmful Content Directed at Minors

  • Bullying, harassment, or intimidation of minors
  • Content that encourages self-harm, eating disorders, or dangerous behaviour among minors
  • Content designed to recruit minors into harmful groups or ideologies
  • Sharing personal information of minors without consent

Any account found posting, sharing, distributing, or engaging in the above will be permanently banned immediately and reported to law enforcement. We do not issue warnings for these violations.

05   How to Report

If you encounter any content or behaviour on AIRnote that you believe endangers or exploits a child, please report it immediately.

In-App Reporting

Every post, comment, and user profile on AIRnote has a report button. Use it to flag content that violates our child safety standards. Reports are reviewed as a priority.

Direct Safety Contact

For urgent child safety concerns, contact us directly:

Safety Email

trinitycreation.dev@gmail.com

Include as much detail as possible — screenshots, usernames, post links, and any other relevant information. We treat all child safety reports as urgent.

External Reporting

If you believe a child is in immediate danger, contact your local emergency services immediately. Do not wait.

You may also report CSAM directly to national authorities and child protection organisations listed in Section 9.

06   Moderation & Enforcement

AIRnote takes the following enforcement actions for child safety violations:

Immediate Actions

  • Immediate removal of all violating content
  • Immediate suspension of the account pending investigation
  • Preservation of all relevant data for law enforcement purposes

Permanent Actions

  • Permanent ban of the account with no possibility of reinstatement
  • Permanent ban of associated email addresses and devices where identifiable
  • Reporting of the violation and user information to law enforcement and child protection authorities

Reporting to Authorities

Trinity Creation will report confirmed or suspected CSAM and child exploitation to:

  • The National Center for Missing and Exploited Children (NCMEC) CyberTipline where applicable
  • Relevant Indian law enforcement authorities under the Protection of Children from Sexual Offences (POCSO) Act, 2012
  • Any other relevant national or international child protection authority

We are legally required under Indian law (POCSO Act, 2012, Section 19) and under Google Play policies to report child sexual abuse material to authorities. We will comply with this obligation without exception.

07   Cooperation With Authorities

AIRnote and Trinity Creation will cooperate fully with:

  • Indian law enforcement agencies investigating child safety violations
  • International law enforcement agencies where applicable
  • Child protection organisations and NGOs
  • Court orders and legal processes related to child safety

We will preserve and provide account data, content, IP addresses, device information, and any other relevant information to authorised law enforcement agencies upon valid legal request.

Cooperation with child safety investigations is not subject to user consent or our standard data protection obligations. Child safety takes absolute priority.

08   Parental Guidance

AIRnote is a public real-time platform. Posts are visible to all users. We strongly encourage parents and guardians to:

  • Review AIRnote with their child before allowing them to use it
  • Discuss safe online behaviour and what to share publicly
  • Remind minors never to share their precise home address, school name, or daily routine publicly
  • Remind minors never to agree to meet strangers they have interacted with online
  • Monitor their child's use of AIRnote regularly
  • Encourage their child to report any uncomfortable interactions immediately

If your child encounters anything on AIRnote that makes them feel uncomfortable, threatened, or unsafe, please contact us immediately and report the content using the in-app reporting tool.
