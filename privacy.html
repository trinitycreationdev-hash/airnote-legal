Privacy Policy for AIRnote

Effective Date: 01 April 2026

AIRnote is a community-based platform where users create and join communities (Books) and share real-life experiences through notes.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Email address
  • User ID
  • Name / username
  • Profile photo
  • Bio (optional)

User Content

  • Notes (text, images, links)
  • Books (communities) you create
  • Tags and categories

Community Activity

  • Books you join
  • Users you follow
  • Likes and reactions
  • Reports submitted

Technical Data

  • Device information
  • Usage logs
  • Crash reports

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Create and manage your account
  • Display your profile and content
  • Enable communities (Books) and interactions
  • Show notes to relevant users
  • Improve performance and features
  • Maintain safety and prevent misuse

We do NOT sell your personal data.

3. Content Visibility

AIRnote is a social platform:

  • Your notes may be visible to other users
  • Content posted inside Books may be visible publicly or to members
  • Your profile information may be visible to others

4. Data Storage and Security

We use trusted services:

  • Firebase Authentication (login)
  • Firebase Firestore (data storage)
  • Firebase Storage (media files)

We follow industry-standard practices but cannot guarantee 100% security.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with advertisers.

Your public content may be visible inside the app.

We may share information if required by law.

6. Your Rights

  • You can update your profile anytime
  • You can delete your account
  • You can remove your content

7. Data Retention

When you delete your account, your data is removed within a reasonable time (typically within 7 days), unless required for legal or security purposes.

8. Children's Privacy

AIRnote is intended for users aged 13 and above.

Accounts found below this age may be removed.

9. External Links

Notes may contain links to third-party websites.

We are not responsible for external content or privacy practices.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time.
