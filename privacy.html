AIRnote Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 01 April 2026

AIRnote is an interest-based lifestyle and knowledge-sharing platform where users share real-life experiences through notes, topics, collections, images, and discussions.


This Privacy Policy explains how AIRnote collects, uses, stores, and protects your information when you use the app.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Email address
  • Username
  • User ID
  • Profile image
  • Bio or profile description

Content You Create

  • Notes you post
  • Images uploaded to notes
  • Links added inside notes
  • Topics and interests connected to notes
  • Pages and collections you create
  • Comments and replies
  • Likes and interactions

Interest & Discovery Data

  • Topics/interests you select
  • Topics you interact with
  • Notes you engage with
  • Notification preferences

Technical Information

  • Device information
  • App usage analytics
  • Crash reports
  • Performance diagnostics
  • Log data

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Create and manage accounts
  • Personalize recommendations and feeds
  • Show topic-based content
  • Enable comments, replies, and interactions
  • Send in-app notifications
  • Improve AIRnote features and experience
  • Prevent abuse, spam, and harmful activity
  • Moderate reported content
  • Maintain platform security and stability

We do NOT sell your personal data.

3. Public Content Visibility

AIRnote is a public social platform.


  • Notes may be visible to other users
  • Usernames and profiles may appear publicly
  • Comments and replies may be public
  • Topics attached to notes may be visible
  • Public pages and collections may appear in discovery

Users should avoid posting sensitive personal information publicly.

4. Interest-Based Recommendations

AIRnote uses interests and topic systems to personalize user experience.


  • Feeds may be personalized based on selected interests
  • Explore recommendations may depend on interactions
  • Users may receive notifications about relevant topics
  • Topic activity may improve content discovery

5. Notifications

AIRnote may generate in-app notifications for:


  • New notes in topics you follow
  • Comments on your notes
  • Replies to your comments
  • Moderation or safety updates

Notification data is stored securely within your account.

6. Data Storage & Security

AIRnote uses trusted third-party services including:


  • Firebase Authentication
  • Firebase Firestore
  • Firebase Storage

We implement reasonable safeguards to protect user information. However, no online service can guarantee complete security.

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent personal information.


Limited data may be shared only:


  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect users and platform security
  • With infrastructure providers required to operate AIRnote

8. User Rights & Controls

  • Edit profile information
  • Delete notes and comments
  • Remove uploaded content
  • Manage interests and topics
  • Delete account
  • Report users or content

9. Data Retention

AIRnote retains account and content data while accounts remain active.


Deleted account data may remain temporarily in backups, moderation systems, or security systems where legally required.

10. Children's Privacy

AIRnote is intended for users aged 13 and above.


We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

11. External Links

Notes may contain links to third-party websites or services. AIRnote is not responsible for their content or privacy practices.

12. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.


Continued use of AIRnote after updates means you accept the revised policy.
