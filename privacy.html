01   About AIRnote

AIRnote is a real-time experience sharing platform operated by Trinity Creation. It allows users to share what they are currently seeing, experiencing, and feeling — organised by location and topic — so others nearby or around the world can discover honest, immediate, real-life moments.

By downloading, accessing, or using AIRnote, you agree to the collection and use of your information as described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, do not use the app.

Governing Law. This policy is governed by the laws of India. Any disputes arising from the use of AIRnote shall be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts in Andhra Pradesh, India.

02   Information We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to provide and improve AIRnote.

Account Information

  • Email address
  • Username and display name
  • Profile photo (if provided)
  • Bio or profile description (if provided)
  • Unique user ID generated on registration

Content You Post

  • Text posts — what you are seeing, experiencing, or feeling
  • Images attached to posts
  • Location text entered manually by you (e.g. "Nandyal, AP")
  • Comments and replies
  • Likes and interactions with other posts

Usage & Technical Data

  • Device type, operating system, and app version
  • App usage patterns and session duration
  • Crash reports and performance diagnostics
  • Log data including timestamps of activity

We do not collect precise GPS location. Location on AIRnote is entered manually by you as plain text. We do not access your device's GPS, background location, or location services at any time.

04   Public Content & Visibility

AIRnote is a public platform. By posting content, you understand and agree that:

  • Your posts, including text and images, may be visible to all users of AIRnote
  • Your username and profile may appear publicly alongside your posts
  • Your manually entered location text is attached to your posts and visible to others
  • Comments and replies you make may be visible publicly
  • Likes and interactions may be visible to other users

Important. Do not post sensitive personal information such as your home address, phone number, financial details, government ID numbers, or any information you would not want visible to the public. AIRnote cannot be held responsible for information you voluntarily make public on the platform.

05   Location Information

AIRnote uses a manual location system. You type your location when creating a post. This text is stored as part of your post and used to show your post in the relevant nearby feed.

  • Location entry is entirely optional — you choose what to share
  • We do not access your device GPS or location services
  • We do not track your real-time physical location
  • Location text you enter is public and visible to other users
  • You may omit location entirely when posting

06   Data Storage & Security

AIRnote uses the following trusted infrastructure providers to store and process data:

  • Firebase Authentication — secure account login and identity management
  • Firebase Firestore — database storage for posts, profiles, and interactions
  • Firebase Storage — storage for images and media uploaded by users

These services are operated by Google LLC and are subject to Google's own privacy and security standards.

Security Limitation. We implement commercially reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your information. You use AIRnote at your own risk in this regard.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for advertising or commercial purposes.

08   Data Retention

We retain your account data and content for as long as your account remains active or as necessary to provide the service.

  • Active accounts. Data is retained while your account exists.
  • Deleted accounts. Upon account deletion, your personal data will be removed from active systems within a reasonable period. Residual copies may remain temporarily in encrypted backups, security logs, or where retention is required by law.
  • Deleted posts. Content you delete is removed from public view immediately. Removal from all backup systems may take additional time.
