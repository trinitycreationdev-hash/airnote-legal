Privacy Policy for AIRnote

Effective Date: 24/01/2026

AIRnote ("we", "our", or "us") operates the AIRnote mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use AIRnote.

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

When you create an account, we may collect:

  • Email address (for authentication)
  • User ID
  • Username
  • Profile photo
  • Cover photo
  • Bio (if provided)

User Content

When using AIRnote, you may share:

  • Notes
  • Images
  • Links
  • Tags and categories

This content may be publicly visible to other users inside the app.

Interactions

  • Likes and unlikes (useful / not useful)
  • Reports submitted on notes

Technical Information

We may automatically collect:

  • Device information
  • App usage logs
  • Crash and performance data

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Display your profile and notes
  • Allow users to interact with content
  • Improve app performance and features
  • Maintain safety and prevent misuse

We do not sell your personal data to anyone.

3. Data Storage and Security

AIRnote uses trusted third-party services such as:

  • Firebase Authentication
  • Firebase Firestore
  • Firebase Storage

These services follow industry-standard security practices. However, no system can be guaranteed to be 100% secure.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal data with advertisers or marketing companies.

Your public profile and notes may be visible to other users of the app.

We may disclose information if required by law or to protect the safety of users and the platform.

5. Children's Privacy

AIRnote is intended for users aged 13 and above.

We do not knowingly allow children under 13 to use the app.

If a user is found to be under 13, their account may be removed.

Users can report accounts suspected to belong to children under 13.

6. Account Deletion

You can delete your account from within the app at any time.

When you delete your account:

  • Your profile information is removed
  • Your notes and uploaded content are deleted
  • Your authentication data is removed

All personal data will be permanently removed from our servers within a reasonable time, typically within 7 days, unless legal obligations require longer retention.

7. Reporting Content

Users can report notes that violate community guidelines.

Reported content may be reviewed and removed if necessary.

8. External Links

Notes may contain links to third-party websites.

AIRnote does not control or take responsibility for external websites, their content, or privacy practices.

9. Free App & No Ads

AIRnote is completely free to use.

There are currently no paid features and no advertisements in the app.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.

When we do, we will update the effective date at the top of this page.
