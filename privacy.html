Privacy Policy for AIRnote

Effective Date: 01/04/2026

AIRnote ("we", "our", or "us") operates the AIRnote mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Email address
  • User ID
  • Name / Username
  • Profile photo
  • Bio (optional)

User Content

  • Notes (text, images, links)
  • Tags and categories
  • Books (communities) you create

Community Activity

  • Books you join
  • Users you follow
  • Likes / reactions (useful / not useful)
  • Reports submitted

Technical Data

  • Device information
  • Usage logs
  • Crash reports

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Create and manage your account
  • Display your profile and content
  • Show notes inside communities (Books)
  • Enable interactions (likes, follows, joining books)
  • Improve performance and features
  • Maintain safety and prevent misuse

We do NOT sell your personal data.

3. Visibility of Content

AIRnote is a community-based platform.

  • Notes may be visible to other users
  • Content posted in Books (communities) may be visible to members or publicly depending on the Book
  • Your profile information may be visible to other users

4. Data Storage and Security

We use secure third-party services:

  • Firebase Authentication
  • Firebase Firestore
  • Firebase Storage

We follow industry standards, but no system is 100% secure.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with advertisers.

Your public content may be visible inside the app.

We may share data if required by law.

6. Children's Privacy

AIRnote is intended for users aged 13 and above.

7. Account Deletion

You can delete your account anytime.

  • Your profile and notes will be removed
  • Your data will be deleted within a reasonable period

8. External Links

Notes may contain links to third-party websites.

We are not responsible for external content.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Changes will be reflected with a new effective date.
