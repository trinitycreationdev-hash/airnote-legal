Privacy Policy for AIRnote

Effective Date: 01 April 2026

AIRnote is a community-based social platform where users create and join communities ("Books") and share real-life experiences through notes.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use AIRnote.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Email address
  • User ID
  • Name / username
  • Profile photo
  • Bio (optional)

User Content

  • Notes (text, images, links)
  • Books (communities) you create
  • Tags and categories

Private Messaging

  • Messages you send and receive (text, images, links)
  • Conversation metadata (timestamps, participants)
  • Reports related to conversations

Community Activity

  • Books you join
  • Users you follow
  • Likes and reactions
  • Reports submitted

Technical Data

  • Device information
  • App usage data
  • Log data and crash reports

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Create and manage your account
  • Display your profile and content
  • Enable communities (Books) and interactions
  • Enable private messaging between users
  • Show relevant content and improve user experience
  • Monitor, detect, and prevent abuse or misuse
  • Review reported content and conversations for moderation
  • Improve app performance and features

We do NOT sell your personal data.

3. Content Visibility

AIRnote is a social platform:

  • Your notes may be visible to other users
  • Content inside Books may be public or limited to members
  • Your profile information may be visible to others
  • Private messages are only visible to participants involved in the conversation

4. Private Messaging & Safety

  • Users can communicate through private chats within the app
  • You can delete messages or entire conversations
  • You can block other users to prevent further interaction
  • You can report conversations or users for violations

Reported conversations may be reviewed by AIRnote to enforce community guidelines and ensure platform safety.

Even if messages are deleted, some data may be temporarily retained for moderation, security, or legal compliance.

5. Data Storage and Security

We use trusted third-party services:

  • Firebase Authentication (account management)
  • Firebase Firestore (database)
  • Firebase Storage (media files)

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal data.

We may share data only in the following cases:

  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To enforce our policies and protect users
  • With service providers (such as Firebase) required to operate the app

Private messages are not shared with third parties except when required for moderation or legal reasons.

7. Your Rights

  • Update or edit your profile information
  • Delete your content at any time
  • Delete your account
  • Block or report other users

8. Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active.

When you delete your account, your data is removed within a reasonable period (typically within 7 days), unless retention is required for legal, security, or abuse prevention purposes.

9. Children's Privacy

AIRnote is intended for users aged 13 and above.

We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. Accounts found violating this may be removed.

10. External Links

Notes may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for their content or privacy practices.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of AIRnote means you accept the updated policy.
